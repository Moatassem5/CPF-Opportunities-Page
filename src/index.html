Opportunities

At Climatic Peace, we believe in the power of youth to lead the charge against climate challenges. Explore a range of programs, exchange opportunities, and resources designed to empower young changemakers worldwide.

Sustainable Future Exchange Program

Join a six-month international program where youth from around the globe collaborate on sustainability projects. Learn from experts in renewable energy, conservation, and policy-making while working on real-world challenges. Build a global network of changemakers and gain hands-on experience in climate action.

  • • Eligibility: Youth aged 18–30
  • • Duration: 6 months (Virtual and In-Person).
  • • Deadline to Apply: March 15, 2024.

Climate Leadership Bootcamp

Participate in an intensive 5-day workshop designed to equip you with leadership, public speaking, and advocacy skills for climate action. Ideal for young activists, students, and community leaders ready to make a difference.

  • • Format: Online (Zoom).
  • • Dates: February 12–16, 2024.
  • • Fee: Free (limited spots).

Youth Climate Fellowship

A prestigious 12-month program providing funding, mentorship, and global exposure for innovative youth-led climate projects. Fellows will work closely with sustainability experts and receive up to $10,000 to implement their ideas.

  • • Eligibility: Young leaders (18–30) with a track record in climate advocacy or innovation.
  • • Benefits: Funding, expert mentorship, and networking opportunities.
  • • Application Deadline: April 1, 2024.

Community Outreach Volunteer

Help us engage local communities in sustainability efforts. As a volunteer, you’ll organize events, educate residents on eco-friendly practices, and contribute to grassroots climate action initiatives.

  • • Commitment: 4–6 hours per week.
  • • Duration: Flexible (minimum 3 months).
  • • Location: Virtual or In-Person (depending on your region).
